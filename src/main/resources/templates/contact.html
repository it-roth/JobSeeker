Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button and choose whether you're a job seeker or employer. Fill out the required information and you're ready to go!

Is JobSeek free to use?

Yes! Job seekers can browse and apply to jobs completely free. Employers have free and premium posting options available.

How do I apply for a job?

Create an account, complete your profile, browse available jobs, and click "Apply Now" on any position that interests you.

Can I edit my application?

You can update your profile anytime, but submitted applications cannot be edited. Make sure to review before submitting!

How long do job postings stay active?

Job postings remain active for 30 days by default. Employers can renew or remove postings at any time.

How do I contact an employer?

Submit your application through our platform. If selected, the employer will contact you directly using the information in your profile.
